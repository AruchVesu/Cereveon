# Isolated Pawn

## What it is
A pawn with no friendly pawns on adjacent files.

## Why it matters
It can be weak but also dynamic.

## Typical signs
- Pawn on d4 or d5 with no neighbors

## Common mistakes
- Defending passively
- Trading attacking pieces

## How Stockfish sees it
Isolated pawns are liabilities in endgames, assets in middlegames.

## Practical advice
Use activity and initiative to compensate.
