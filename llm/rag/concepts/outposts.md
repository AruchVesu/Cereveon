# Outposts

## What it is
Strong squares protected by pawns and hard to challenge.

## Why it matters
Outposts provide stable positions for knights or bishops.

## Typical signs
- Knight on a square unchallenged by pawns
- Central or advanced square

## Common mistakes
- Allowing permanent enemy outposts

## How Stockfish sees it
Outposts increase piece value significantly.

## Practical advice
Either prevent or challenge outposts early.
