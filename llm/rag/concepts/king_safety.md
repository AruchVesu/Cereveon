# King Safety

## What it is
How protected the king is from direct attack.

## Why it matters
An exposed king can lose the game regardless of material.

## Typical signs
- King stuck in the center
- Open files near the king
- Missing pawn shield

## Common mistakes
- Delaying castling without reason
- Weakening pawn cover

## How Stockfish sees it
King exposure is punished heavily, especially with active enemy pieces.

## Practical advice
Castle early and avoid unnecessary pawn moves near the king.
