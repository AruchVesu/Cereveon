# Initiative

## What it is
The ability to make threats that force the opponent to respond.

## Why it matters
Initiative dictates the pace and flow of the game.

## Typical signs
- Forcing moves
- Opponent reacting defensively
- Continuous threats

## Common mistakes
- Giving up initiative for small material gains
- Passive defense

## How Stockfish sees it
Engines value initiative even over material in dynamic positions.

## Practical advice
When ahead in activity, keep applying pressure.
