# Tempo

## What it is
A single move or unit of time.

## Why it matters
Losing tempo can allow the opponent to improve their position.

## Typical signs
- Forced responses
- Gaining free development moves

## Common mistakes
- Unnecessary pawn moves
- Repeating moves without purpose

## How Stockfish sees it
Engines seek maximum efficiency per move.

## Practical advice
Ask what each move threatens or improves.
