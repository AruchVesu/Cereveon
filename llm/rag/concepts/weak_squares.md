# Weak Squares

## What it is
Squares that cannot be defended by pawns.

## Why it matters
They become permanent outposts for enemy pieces.

## Typical signs
- Pawn advances creating holes
- Squares near the king

## Common mistakes
- Pawn pushes without considering consequences

## How Stockfish sees it
Weak squares are long-term strategic liabilities.

## Practical advice
Avoid pawn moves that create irreversible weaknesses.
