# Development

## What it is
Bringing pieces from their starting squares to active positions.

## Why it matters
Developed pieces coordinate better and enable both attack and defense.

## Typical signs
- Minor pieces off the back rank
- Rooks connected
- King castled

## Common mistakes
- Moving the same piece multiple times early
- Ignoring development to chase material

## How Stockfish sees it
Engines heavily penalize lagging development, especially with open positions.

## Practical advice
Develop all minor pieces before launching attacks.
