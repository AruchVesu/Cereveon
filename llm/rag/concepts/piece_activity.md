# Piece Activity

## What it is
How effective and mobile your pieces are.

## Why it matters
Active pieces create threats and control key squares.

## Typical signs
- Pieces on open files/diagonals
- Few blocked pieces

## Common mistakes
- Passive piece placement
- Blocking own pieces

## How Stockfish sees it
Active pieces increase evaluation even without immediate tactics.

## Practical advice
Improve worst-placed piece before creating new plans.
