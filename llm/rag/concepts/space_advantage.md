# Space Advantage

## What it is
Having more territory on the board.

## Why it matters
Space limits opponent mobility and enables attacks.

## Typical signs
- Advanced pawns
- Cramped opponent position

## Common mistakes
- Overextending pawns
- Ignoring counterplay

## How Stockfish sees it
Space is valued when it can be supported.

## Practical advice
Use space to maneuver pieces, not just push pawns.
